Description

Supposedly Wonderful Future is a sci-fi narrative game that plays like a cross between a point-and-click adventure, a text-heavy RPG, and a visual novel. A young man is invited to skip his own death by relocating to 2048 - if only he does some work for the world's biggest corporation first...

History

Made by one person in 4 years on a zero budget, SWFuture was born out of fascination with video games as a storytelling medium; a project with a singular focus where everything serves one purpose: to tell a meaningful, thought-provoking, and emotional story.

Features

  • Approximate word count: 125,000. Estimated play time: 4 to 8 hours.
  • 5 chapters + prologue, united yet diverse enough to work on their own.
  • Extensive UI customization options to keep text as readable for everyone as possible.
  • Modest system requirements (a decade-old PC should do).
  • Enough jokes to forget about the daily horrors of human condition. For a bit. Maybe.
  • There are some choices, but they don't really matter. Or do they?
